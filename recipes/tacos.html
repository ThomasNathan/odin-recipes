Thomas Tacos

Tacos that will make you forget all your problems.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Brown beef
  2. Add water and taco seasoning, boil
  3. Spread butter on tortillas
  4. Heat Tortilla on skillet
  5. Add meat to taco
